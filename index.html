Anaya Food Industry – The Taste of Pakistan

From Karachi to Kashmir, Anaya Food Industry is shaping the future of snacks, biscuits, and confectionery. We bring authentic flavors, trusted quality, and innovation that reflect Pakistan's spirit.

Who We Are

At Anaya Food Industry, we don't just make food – we create experiences. Founded in Karachi's DHA Phase 2, our vision is to craft snacks that represent Pakistan's vibrant taste and global standards. From Crunchies Chips to Chocomo Biscuits, every product is a story of care and innovation.

Our Flagship Products

  • Crunchies Chips – bold flavors, inspired by Pakistan's love for crispy snacks
  • Chocomo Biscuits – delicious chocolate-filled biscuits loved by kids & families
  • Snack Innovations – exciting new tastes with every season
